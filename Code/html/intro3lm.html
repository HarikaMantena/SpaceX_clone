SEPTEMBER 8, 2023

STARLINK MISSION

On Friday, September 8 at 11:12 p.m. ET, Falcon 9 launched 22 Starlink satellites to low-Earth orbit from from Space Launch Complex 40 (SLC-40) at Cape Canaveral Space Force Station in Florida.

This was the seventh flight for the first stage booster supporting this mission, which previously launched CRS-26, OneWeb Launch 16, Intelsat IS-40e, and now four Starlink missions.
