SEPTEMBER 11 2023

STARLINK MISSION

On Monday, September 11 at 11:57 p.m. PT, Falcon 9 launched 21 Starlink satellites to low-Earth orbit from from Space Launch Complex 4 East (SLC-4E) at Vandenberg Space Force Base in California.

This was the 11th flight for the first stage booster supporting this mission, which previously launched NROL-87, NROL-85, SARah-1, SWOT, Transporter-8, and now six Starlink missions.
